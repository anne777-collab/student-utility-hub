About Us

Tools built to make student life simpler.

Student Utility Hub provides free tools like CGPA calculator, percentage calculator, and more, all in a clean, fast, and mobile-friendly interface.

What We Do

This website is designed to help students quickly solve everyday academic calculations without needing complicated spreadsheets or extra apps. Everything is available in one place and works directly in your browser.

Our Mission

Helping students with simple and fast tools. We believe useful educational utilities should be easy to access, easy to understand, and easy to trust.

Built With Students in Mind

Every page is designed to stay lightweight, readable, and responsive so students can use the site comfortably on laptops, tablets, or phones.
