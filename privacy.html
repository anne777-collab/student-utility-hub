Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters at Student Utility Hub.

This page explains how Student Utility Hub handles privacy, cookies, and third-party services in a simple and transparent way.

Information We Collect

Student Utility Hub does not directly collect personal data from users through the calculators. The tools run in the browser and do not require account creation.

This website may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, improving the user experience, and showing relevant advertisements.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to deliver and measure ads based on visits to this and other websites.

Data Safety

We aim to keep the website safe and lightweight. Since we do not directly request personal account information for calculator use, your interaction remains simple and low risk.

User Awareness

By using Student Utility Hub, you understand that some third-party services may process technical usage information according to their own privacy policies.
